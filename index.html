The New NicholasNasibyan.com
Working directly with a client to create their vision was an experience that taught me a lot about web development. NicholasNasibyan.com is a product of a joint effort between myself and Nicholas Nasibyan of Erie, Pennsylvania, an up and coming professional pianist. Nick and I scraped various versions of the website, rolling back features and tweaking styles until we had the perfect final product. The website features a dynamic backend written in PHP with a login system the Nicholas can use to log in and edit his website in the browser, with a simple and user-friendly interface. The site also features an email system that allows Nicholas's fans to get in touch with him.
